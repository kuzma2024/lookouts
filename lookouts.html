About Lookouts

Lookouts is an engaging tower defense strategy game that challenges players to defend their base against relentless waves of enemies. Set in a beautifully crafted world, you'll need to strategically place defensive structures and manage resources to survive increasingly difficult attacks.

This browser-based game combines classic tower defense mechanics with innovative gameplay elements, offering both newcomers and veterans of the genre an exciting and challenging experience. Plan your defenses, upgrade your towers, and adapt your strategy as new enemy types emerge.
